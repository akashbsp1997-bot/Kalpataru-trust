Rooted in Service

Kalpataru Sevasikshyashram is the realization of Parshuram Panda's lifelong commitment to grassroots service. Believing that education is the ultimate tool for liberation, he established this institution to serve the marginalized communities of Odisha.

"True service begins where the road ends. Every child deserves the light of knowledge."

Under his guidance, we have grown from a small study circle into a comprehensive center for welfare and vocational growth, focusing on holistic development.
